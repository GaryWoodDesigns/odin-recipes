Pasta

Description

This Pasta dish is quick and simple. It's tasty, healthy and tastes bloody great!

It's also a good meal to create for meal prep. It can be frozen and re-heated, whislt still tasting great!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Herbs of your choice

Steps

  1. Boil a pan of water and start cooking the pasta
  2. Chop up the onions and peppers
  3. Fry the onions and peppers
  4. Add the chopped tomates
  5. Mix in the herbs
  6. Add the pasta into the sauce
  7. Serve in a bowl
